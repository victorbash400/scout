# 🕵️ SCOUT - System Blueprint

## 🎯 **WHAT THIS IS**
AI system that takes business plans and outputs GO/NO-GO decisions with comprehensive market intelligence reports.

---

## 🧠 **7-AGENT SYSTEM**

### **1. PLANNER AGENT** 📋
**Input:** Business plan (PDF/text/upload)
**Job:** Convert plan into structured research to-do list
**Output:** Research brief with specific tasks for each specialist agent
```
TO-DO LIST EXAMPLE:
SCOUT RESEARCH BRIEF - TO-DO LIST
Business: Healthy Meal Delivery Service - Austin, TX
Generated by: PLANNER AGENT

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMPETITION AGENT TASKS:

Direct Competitor Analysis:
- Identify all meal delivery services operating in Austin (DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub, etc.)
- Map healthy food delivery specialists (Freshly, Factor, HelloFresh, etc.)
- Analyze local Austin food delivery startups and regional players
- Document each competitor's service model, delivery areas, and operational scale

Pricing Intelligence:
- Compile delivery fees for all major competitors
- Analyze meal pricing across different service tiers
- Document subscription vs. one-time order pricing models  
- Map promotional strategies and discount structures
- Calculate average order values and customer acquisition costs

Market Positioning:
- Create competitor positioning matrix (price vs. quality vs. convenience)
- Identify underserved market segments and positioning gaps
- Analyze marketing messages and brand differentiation strategies
- Document customer review patterns and satisfaction scores
- Map competitor strengths, weaknesses, and vulnerability points

Financial Intelligence:
- Research competitor funding rounds, valuations, and financial health
- Analyze business model sustainability and profitability indicators
- Document expansion patterns and market share data
- Investigate supply chain partnerships and cost structures

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

MARKET AGENT TASKS:

Customer Demographics:
- Profile Austin population demographics (age, income, lifestyle)
- Identify health-conscious consumer segments and sizing
- Map millennial and Gen-Z food ordering behaviors
- Analyze geographic distribution of target customers across Austin
- Research correlation between income levels and healthy food spending

Market Sizing:
- Calculate Total Addressable Market (TAM) for food delivery in Austin
- Determine Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM) for healthy meal delivery
- Estimate Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) for new entrant
- Project market growth rates over 3-5 year period
- Analyze seasonal demand patterns and cyclical trends

Demand Validation:
- Research Google search trends for "healthy meal delivery Austin"
- Analyze social media sentiment around healthy eating in Austin
- Document food truck and restaurant trends indicating demand
- Survey university populations (UT, ACC) for delivery preferences
- Investigate corporate catering opportunities for healthy meals

Geographic Analysis:
- Map Austin neighborhoods by delivery demand and demographics
- Identify optimal launch zones based on target customer concentration
- Analyze traffic patterns and delivery logistics challenges
- Research competitor service coverage gaps
- Document local food regulations and delivery infrastructure

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

FINANCIAL AGENT TASKS:

Unit Economics Modeling:
- Calculate customer acquisition cost (CAC) for meal delivery
- Model customer lifetime value (CLV) for subscription vs. one-time orders
- Analyze contribution margins for different meal price points
- Document delivery cost per order including driver wages and fuel
- Model kitchen operational costs and food waste percentages

Pricing Strategy:
- Test pricing sensitivity across different customer segments
- Analyze optimal meal pricing vs. competitor benchmarks
- Model subscription pricing tiers and retention rates
- Calculate break-even pricing for sustainable operations
- Design promotional pricing strategy for customer acquisition

Financial Projections:
- Build 5-year revenue projections with multiple growth scenarios
- Model cash flow requirements for first 24 months of operations
- Calculate break-even timeline under different customer acquisition rates
- Project funding requirements for scaling operations
- Analyze working capital needs and seasonal cash flow impacts

Investment Analysis:
- Research typical startup funding rounds for meal delivery companies
- Calculate return on investment scenarios for potential investors
- Model exit valuation multiples based on comparable companies
- Analyze risk-adjusted returns and payback periods
- Document capital efficiency metrics vs. industry benchmarks

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

RISK AGENT TASKS:

Regulatory Compliance:
- Research Texas food service licensing requirements
- Document Austin city permits needed for food delivery operations
- Analyze driver classification laws and employment regulations
- Investigate food safety and handling compliance requirements
- Research insurance requirements for food delivery businesses

Market Risks:
- Analyze economic recession impact on food delivery demand
- Document seasonal demand fluctuations and mitigation strategies
- Research potential market saturation and competitive response risks
- Evaluate dependency on third-party delivery platforms
- Analyze customer churn risks and retention challenges

Operational Risks:
- Document food supply chain vulnerabilities and backup suppliers
- Analyze driver shortage risks and wage inflation pressures
- Research kitchen capacity constraints and scaling challenges
- Evaluate technology platform reliability and backup systems
- Investigate food safety incident risks and crisis management

Competitive Threats:
- Analyze risk of major competitors (DoorDash, Uber) entering healthy segment
- Document potential for existing healthy meal companies expanding to Austin
- Research venture capital funding trends in meal delivery space
- Evaluate risk of new technology disrupting traditional delivery model
- Analyze potential for grocery delivery services competing directly

Strategic Risks:
- Document customer acquisition cost inflation risks
- Analyze brand differentiation sustainability over time
- Research intellectual property risks and trademark conflicts
- Evaluate key personnel dependency and succession planning
- Investigate potential regulatory changes affecting gig economy

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SYNTHESIS REQUIREMENTS:

Executive Summary:
- Consolidate all findings into GO/NO-GO recommendation
- Calculate overall market opportunity score (1-100)
- Assign risk level rating (Low/Medium/High) with justification
- Project financial viability timeline and confidence intervals

Strategic Recommendations:
- Prioritize optimal market entry strategy based on all agent findings
- Recommend pricing strategy balancing competitiveness and profitability
- Suggest geographic launch sequence and expansion timeline
- Provide 90-day action plan with specific milestones and metrics

Visual Intelligence:
- Create competitor positioning map with pricing and market share data
- Generate market sizing charts with growth projections
- Build financial model dashboard with key metrics visualization
- Develop risk assessment matrix with probability and impact scoring

### **2. ORCHESTRATOR AGENT** 🎛️
**Input:** Research brief from Planner
**Job:** Deploy and coordinate 4 specialist agents
**Output:** Progress monitoring and agent coordination

### **3. COMPETITION AGENT** 🥊
**Input:** Competition research tasks
**Job:** Deep competitor intelligence gathering
**Output:** Massive competitor dataset including:
- Complete competitor profiles (50+ companies)
- Pricing matrices and business models
- Market share analysis and positioning maps
- Competitive threat assessments
- SWOT analysis for each major competitor

### **4. MARKET AGENT** 👥
**Input:** Market research tasks  
**Job:** Comprehensive market and customer analysis
**Output:** Extensive market intelligence including:
- Detailed demographic breakdowns and customer personas
- Complete TAM/SAM/SOM calculations with methodology
- Market growth trends and projections (5-year outlook)
- Geographic opportunity mapping
- Consumer behavior patterns and demand validation data

### **5. FINANCIAL AGENT** 💰
**Input:** Financial analysis tasks
**Job:** Complete business model and financial viability analysis
**Output:** Comprehensive financial intelligence including:
- Detailed unit economics models
- Multiple pricing scenario analyses
- Cash flow projections and break-even timelines
- Funding requirement calculations with burn rate analysis
- Sensitivity analysis and financial risk assessments

### **6. RISK AGENT** ⚠️
**Input:** Risk assessment tasks
**Job:** Identify and analyze all potential threats
**Output:** Complete risk intelligence including:
- Regulatory compliance requirements and costs
- Market risk scenarios and probability assessments
- Operational risk analysis (supply chain, hiring, scaling)
- Competitive threat modeling
- Legal and IP risk assessments

### **7. SYNTHESIS AGENT** 📊
**Input:** All specialist agent outputs
**Job:** Transform raw intelligence into executive reports
**Output:** Final Scout Report with tables, graphs, charts, and GO/NO-GO recommendation

## 🏗️ **TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE**

### **AWS Services**
- **Amazon Bedrock:** All LLM calls (Claude-3.5-Sonnet)
- **Strands SDK:** Agent development framework
- **Amazon Bedrock AgentCore:** Agent deployment and cloud tools (browser, code interpreter, memory)
- **Amazon S3:** Document storage
- **Amazon CloudWatch:** Monitoring

### **Agent Flow**
```
USER UPLOAD → PLANNER → ORCHESTRATOR → [4 AGENTS PARALLEL] → SYNTHESIS → REPORT

## 🎯 **INPUTS & OUTPUTS**

### **System Input**
- Business plan document (any format)
- User answers to clarifying questions

### **Agent Outputs**
Each specialist agent produces **massive datasets**, not summary lines:

**Competition Agent:**  competitor analysis with detailed profiles, pricing matrices, market positioning maps

**Market Agent:**  market intelligence report with demographic breakdowns, TAM/SAM calculations, growth projections

**Financial Agent:**  financial model with unit economics, cash flow projections, scenario analyses

**Risk Agent:**  risk assessment with regulatory requirements, threat scenarios, mitigation strategies

### **Final System Output**
- **Executive Dashboard:** GO/NO-GO decision with confidence scores
- **Intelligence Report:** 150+ page comprehensive analysis with charts and graphs
- **Strategic Action Plan:** 90-day roadmap with specific milestones

---

## 🔧 **CORE SPECIFICATIONS**

### **Processing Flow**
1. **Plan Intake:** Planner processes business plan and creates research to-do list
2. **Task Distribution:** Orchestrator deploys specialist agents with specific assignments
3. **Parallel Research:** 4 agents execute autonomous research simultaneously
4. **Intelligence Synthesis:** Synthesis agent combines all findings into executive report
5. **Delivery:** Complete Scout Report with GO/NO-GO recommendation

### **Technology Stack**
- **Agent Framework:** Strands SDK
- **LLM Platform:** Amazon Bedrock (Claude-3.5-Sonnet)
- **Cloud Tools:** AgentCore (browser, code interpreter, memory)
- **Data Storage:** Amazon S3
- **Monitoring:** Amazon CloudWatch
